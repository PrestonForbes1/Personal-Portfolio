My Timeline.

2018

In 2018 I enlisted in the United States Army as an Infantryman. After completing basic combat training at Fort Benning, GA, I was shipped off to my first duty location; Bavaria, Germany. I spent next two and a half years there, working to become the best Soldier I could be.

2020

In 2020 it was time for me to leave Germany and Fort Carson, Colorado was my next stop. This was a huge year for me, I met my wife, got promoted to Sergeant and bought my fist house.

2021

The year I became a Dad. My son Kyler was born just three days after Christmas, being a father has changed every aspect of my life for the better and my motivation is being able to give him more than I had.

2022

The year I took the biggest risk of my life and decided to leave the US Army. I started my cohort with SkillStorm and hope to land a job as a developer.
